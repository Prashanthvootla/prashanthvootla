Welcome to Busch Gardens Tampa Bay

Where thrilling roller coasters, fascinating wildlife, and unforgettable entertainment come together to create an extraordinary experience for all ages. Nestled in the heart of Tampa, Florida, our park offers a unique blend of adventure and exploration, making it the perfect destination for families, thrill-seekers, and animal lovers alike.

Discover a World of Adventure

Thrilling Attractions

Prepare yourself for an adrenaline rush with our world-class roller coasters. From the record-breaking heights of Iron Gwazi to the heart-pounding drops of SheiKra, our rides are designed to excite and exhilarate. Don't miss out on Cheetah Hunt's speedy sprints or Montu's daring inversions – there's a thrill waiting around every corner.

Explore the Wild

Embark on a journey through our expansive animal habitats and get up close with some of the world's most incredible creatures. Wander through the Serengeti Plain and witness free-roaming giraffes and zebras, marvel at the majestic tigers in Jungala, and explore the depths of the African jungle in Edge of Africa. Our animal exhibits offer a rare glimpse into the lives of these magnificent animals in settings that mimic their natural habitats.

Unforgettable Entertainment Busch Gardens Tampa Bay is not just about rides and animals – our live shows and seasonal events add an extra layer of excitement to your visit. Enjoy the dazzling performances of Turn It Up! Ice Show, or laugh along with the antics of our rescued animals in Critter Castaways. And don't miss our special events like Howl-O-Scream during Halloween and Christmas Town during the holiday season. Our park is designed with families in mind. Little ones will love Sesame Street Safari of Fun, where they can meet their favorite characters, enjoy kid-friendly rides, and explore interactive play areas. For a splash of adventure, hop on the Congo River Rapids and experience a wild river journey.

Plan Your Visit Get the most out of your visit by planning ahead. Check our calendar for park hours and special events, purchase tickets online for convenience, and consider adding a Quick Queue pass to skip the lines on your favorite rides. Don’t forget to explore our dining options and shops for a complete Busch Gardens experience. Connect With Us Stay connected with Busch Gardens Tampa Bay by following us on social media and subscribing to our newsletter for the latest news, events, and special offers. We look forward to welcoming you to a world of adventure and excitement.
